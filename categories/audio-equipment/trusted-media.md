## English / 英文

### Tier 1 — Technical Reviews with Measurements / 含量測的技術評測（最高信賴度）
- **Sound On Sound** — soundonsound.com (UK, monthly, deep technical features)
- **Stereophile** — stereophile.com (US, contains measurement lab reports)
- **AudioScienceReview** — audiosciencereview.com (measurement-first community)
- **Resolution Magazine** — resolutionmag.com (pro studio focus)

### Tier 2 — Professional Industry Media / 專業業界媒體（高信賴度）
- ProSoundWeb — prosoundweb.com
- Mix Magazine — mixonline.com
- Electronic Musician — emusician.com
- Recording Magazine — recordingmag.com
- Tape Op — tapeop.com (engineer interviews, real-world focus)
- AES Journal — aes.org/journal (academic, peer-reviewed)

### Tier 3 — General Audio Publications / 一般音頻出版物（中等信賴度）
- Sweetwater — sweetwater.com (US retailer, but has tech articles)
- B&H Explora — bhphotovideo.com/explora
- Gearank — gearank.com

## Exclusion List / 排除清單

The following source types are EXCLUDED from trusted media regardless of category:
以下類型的來源無論品類均被排除：

- Anonymous blog posts without author credentials / 無作者背景的匿名部落格
- E-commerce product descriptions (Amazon, eBay listings) / 電商產品文案
- Social media posts (Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok) / 社群媒體貼文
- AI-generated content without human editorial oversight / 無人工編輯監督的 AI 生成內容
- Press releases without independent editorial addition / 無獨立編輯補充的新聞稿
