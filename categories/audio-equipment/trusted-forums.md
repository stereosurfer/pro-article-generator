## English / 英文

  - 優先：20 樓以上討論串、具名業界人士、技術性內容
- **AudioScienceReview (ASR)** — audiosciencereview.com
  - Priority: threads with actual measurements attached
  - 優先：附有實際量測數據的討論串
- **GroupDIY** — groupdiy.com
  - Focus: electronics, circuit analysis, vintage equipment restoration
  - 重點：電子電路分析、復古設備修復
- **Tape Op Community** — forum.tapeop.com
  - Focus: real engineer workflow, named professionals

## Selection Rules / 篩選規則

Apply these filters when selecting forum posts as sources:
選擇論壇貼文作為來源時，應用以下篩選條件：

| Criterion / 標準 | Requirement / 要求 |
|---|---|
| Thread length / 討論串長度 | > 15 posts preferred, > 5 posts minimum / 優先 >15 樓，最少 >5 樓 |
| Named professionals / 具名業界人士 | At least 1 named professional participant / 至少 1 位具名業界人士參與 |
| Recency / 時效性 | Within 10 years preferred, within 15 years acceptable / 優先 10 年內，接受 15 年內 |
| Technical content / 技術含量 | Must contain technical specifics, not just opinions / 必須包含技術細節，不只是主觀意見 |

**Automatic exclusions / 自動排除：**
- Anonymous one-liner opinions / 匿名單行意見
- Emotional discussions without technical content / 無技術內容的情緒性討論
- Promotional or sponsored posts / 廣告或贊助性質貼文
- Posts where original content is deleted / 原始內容已被刪除的貼文
