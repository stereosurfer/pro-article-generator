## Official Manufacturer Documentation / 原廠官方文件

Search approach: `[brand] [model] site:[official domain] (manual OR "user guide" OR datasheet OR "service manual")`

Priority targets / 優先目標:
- Product pages with downloadable manuals / 含可下載手冊的產品頁
- Service manuals (repair-level documentation) / 維修手冊（維修級文件）
- Engineering white papers / 工程白皮書
- Archived historical product pages (use Wayback Machine if needed) / 歷史產品頁存檔（必要時用 Wayback Machine）
